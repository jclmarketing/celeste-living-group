Home · About

A fiancé & fiancée team, building something that lasts.

We're Bruno and Kirstie — partners in life and in business. Celeste Living Group is our property venture: deal sourcing and flips, run with the seriousness of people who are here for the long game.

Our story

Capable operators, honest about where we are.

We didn't arrive in property pretending to be the most experienced people in the room — because we're not. What we bring is something different: real-world business experience, strong people skills, and the kind of long-term mindset that makes property partnerships actually work.

We're at the beginning of this chapter, and we're not going to dress that up. What we are is prepared, committed, and here for the long run. No shortcuts. No inflated CVs. Just two people doing this properly.

The name Celeste comes from our intention — curated living and experiences that feel considered, calm, and a little bit elevated. That's the same standard we hold ourselves to in every project, every conversation, and every deal we put our name on.

Deal sourcing & property flips

Two services. Same standard.

We keep the offering tight on purpose. Two strands, done properly, with the same values underneath.

01 Deal sourcing

Deals that match your criteria — not ours.

We source compliant, bespoke property deals for serious investors in Birmingham and Staffordshire. Every deal is underwritten on real figures, with honest assumptions and a clear view of the exit.

  • + BTL, BRR, flip and HMO-suitable opportunities
  • + Full deal pack with figures, comparables and exit strategy
  • + PRS-registered, AML-compliant, ICO-registered
  • + We walk on anything that doesn't stack
02 Property flips

Flips with a high-end finish as baseline.

We buy select properties, refurbish them to the standard we'd want to move into ourselves, and bring them back to market with the kind of specification that sells on day one. Fewer projects, done better.

  • + High-end finish as standard — not an upgrade
  • + Planning the exit before we commit to the entry
  • + Trusted local trades — loyalty, not the lowest quote
  • + Open to joint-venture and investor partnerships
