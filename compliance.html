Compliance, done properly.

Property sourcing is regulated — rightly so. Here's what we have in place on our side, and exactly what we need from you as an investor before we can forward you a deal.

What we need from you

Investor onboarding — simple, fast, thorough.

Before we can forward deals, we need to verify who you are and that your funds are legitimate. It's straightforward, it's secure, and it protects both sides. We usually get this done inside a couple of working days.

If any of it feels unfamiliar, that's completely normal — we'll walk you through each step personally.

Get started →
Your onboarding pack

Three things we'll need.

Nothing surprising. Nothing onerous.

01 · Proof of identity

  • A clear, in-date passport or photo driving licence
  • One proof of address from the last 3 months (utility bill, bank statement, council tax)

02 · Proof of funds

  • Recent bank statement showing available funds or
  • Mortgage agreement in principle / lender decision
  • If funds are a gift or inheritance, a short source-of-funds letter

03 · Investor criteria

  • Budget range and deposit available
  • Preferred strategy (BTL, BRR, flip, HMO)
  • Target area and timescale
Why it matters

Compliance isn't red tape — it's trust, in writing.

Every shortcut in sourcing eventually costs somebody. We'd rather take an extra day on onboarding than skip something that protects you, us, or the next investor we work with.

If a sourcer ever tells you the paperwork is "optional" or that they can "start with the deal and do the ID later" — that's your cue to walk away.
