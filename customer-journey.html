Home · How we work

The customer journey, in plain English.

No jargon. No hidden steps. Here's exactly what happens from the first conversation to the first deal — and what we'll need from you at each stage.

01 Intro call

A proper first conversation

Thirty minutes. Relaxed. We learn about your position — what you're looking for, your experience, your timescale — and you learn about us.

What you'll get

A clear sense of whether we're a fit, honest thoughts on your strategy, and zero pressure to commit to anything afterwards.

02 Discovery

Your criteria, pinned down

A short written brief — budget, strategy, area, deposit, timescale, risk appetite. Agreed by both sides and used as the filter on every deal we forward.

What you'll get

A one-page investor brief you keep, and a clear understanding of what we will and won't send your way.

03 Onboarding

Compliance & paperwork

Proof of ID, proof of address, proof of funds and a quick AML check. Usually done inside 48 hours. We help you through every part of it.

What you'll get

A complete onboarding pack, signed agreements on both sides, and confirmation that you're cleared to receive deals.

04 Deal flow

Opportunities that actually stack

You receive deal packs that match your criteria — with real figures, honest assumptions, comparables, and a clear exit. If there's nothing worth sending, we send nothing.

What you'll get

Clear deal packs, honest answers to every question, and the space to say no without pressure.

05 Offer & acquisition

We hand you to the right people

If you want to proceed, we introduce you to a solicitor, broker and (if needed) project manager from our trusted network. We stay close throughout.

What you'll get

A smooth handover, a single point of contact, and regular updates without you having to chase.

06 Ongoing

The relationship, not just the deal

After completion, we stay in touch. We want to know how it performs, what you'd do differently, and what's next. The first deal is rarely the only deal.

What you'll get

A long-term partner, honest feedback loops, and early sight of future opportunities that fit your portfolio.

A few questions answered

Before you book — the things people usually ask.

Do I need experience in property to work with you?

+

No. We work with first-time investors as well as experienced portfolio landlords. What matters more than experience is having a clear idea of what you want, realistic expectations, and funds in place (or a plan to get there).

How do you charge for sourcing?

+

Our fees are transparent and set out in writing before onboarding. We'll walk you through our fee structure on the intro call, along with when it's paid and what it covers. No surprises.

What areas do you source in?

+

Primarily Birmingham and Staffordshire. We stay local on purpose — we walk the streets, know the pricing nuance, and can be on-site quickly when something needs checking.

How long does onboarding take?

+

Typically one to two working days once we have your documents. We'll guide you through every part of it, so there's nothing to figure out on your own.

What if I'm not ready to invest yet?

+

That's fine. Plenty of our conversations start months before someone's ready to move. Book a call, have a chat, and we'll stay in touch until the timing's right.
