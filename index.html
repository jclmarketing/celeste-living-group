What we do

Two strands. One standard.

We find property deals that stack — and we run flips where the finish people expect as an upgrade is simply our baseline. Whether you're an investor looking for a sourced opportunity or a partner looking at a completed flip, the bar doesn't change.

01

Deal sourcing

Compliant, bespoke sourcing across Birmingham and Staffordshire. We know the streets, the pricing nuance, and when to walk away — which happens more often than most sourcers admit.

02

Property flips

Select flips with a high-end finish as standard. We plan the exit before we buy the entry, and we'd rather do fewer, better projects than churn through quick-margin compromises.

Our principles

Six commitments we won't move on.

These aren't tagline words. They're how we'll behave on the boring Tuesdays when nobody's watching.

i

Honesty over hype

No inflated numbers. No unrealistic promises. If a deal doesn't stack on real figures, it doesn't get forwarded, no matter how much we'd like the fee.

ii

Relationships first

This is a people business. Investors, sourcers and trades are long-term partners — not transactions to close and move on from.

iii

Communication is everything

Clear, consistent and proactive. You shouldn't have to chase us for an update, and you shouldn't get a different answer depending on which of us you ask.

iv

High standards as baseline

High-end finish isn't an upgrade — it's the floor. Every project gets the spec we'd want if we were moving in ourselves.

v

Local & loyal

Focused on areas we actually know — Birmingham and Staffordshire — and building a trusted local network of trades and professionals over the long term.

vi

Early stage, serious intent

We're at the beginning of this journey — and we're not pretending otherwise. What we bring is preparation, real-world business experience, and the intention to be here in twenty years.

The Celeste way

“We won't be the loudest in the room. We'd rather be the people you'd be happy to back on the deal after next.”

Property is long. So are the relationships that make it work. Our job is to keep both in good shape — which starts with being clear about what we are, what we're not, and what you can expect from us.

How we work

A clear four-step path, without the fluff.

Every partnership starts with a proper introduction — not a hard sell. Here's the short version. The longer version lives on the how we work page.

01 Intro call

Get to know each other

A relaxed 30-minute conversation. What are you looking for, what are we working on, and is there a fit worth exploring.

02 Discovery

Your criteria, clearly defined

Budget, strategy, area, timescale and risk appetite — written down, agreed, and used as the filter for every deal we forward.

03 Onboarding

Compliance & paperwork

The proper checks — ID, proof of funds, AML. We'd rather do these properly than cut corners and clean up later.

04 Deals

Opportunities that stack

You see deals that match your criteria, with real figures, honest risks and clear next steps. If there's nothing worth sending, we don't send anything.
